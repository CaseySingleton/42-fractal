Q, E - Change color schemes
W, A, S, D - Move camera up, left, down and right respectively
mouse1, num_pad_+ - Zoom camera in
num_pad_- - Zoom camera out
mouse wheel - Zoom camera in and out
up_arrow - Increase iterations
down_arrow - Decrease iterations
space_bar - Toggles mouse input for the julia fractal. Hold space down for a cool effect!
